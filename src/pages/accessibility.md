Supply & Demand is committed to providing an inclusive environment for all guests visiting our establishment. The building is accessible to all persons with disabilities, and we are dedicated to providing the opportunity to equally enjoy our space, services and products that we offer.  Please see the information outlined below or contact us before visiting to answer any of your specialized questions. We will continue to make improvements to the facility’s accessibility.

## Is the entrance acessible?

The entrance of Supply & Demad is accessible, including a push button to open the door.

## Where can I park & drop off?

There are a number of accessible parking spots available on the block where Supply & Demand is located. However if you would like to park temporarily to unload, there is an unloading spot in front of our building you are more than welcome to use.

## Are there accessible washrooms?

We have two universal washrooms available onsite.
